Hi, I'm Kyle.


For the past few years, I worked as a Research Assistant for Skidmore College's Sociology and American Studies departments. While I enjoyed my job, I found I was most passionate about that work - solving research queries and breaking problems apart - when I leveraged those same skills in my free-time to study programming. At the beginning of this year, I decided to commit myself fully to programming and enrolled in Epicodus, a four-month long programming bootcamp.

I'm a recent transplant to Portland, OR and when I'm not programming, I like to explore the city on my bike. I also enjoy making terrariums and throwing cups and bowls on the pottery wheel.
